Region Training

Learn about the National Leadership Seminar (NLS), National Lodge Adviser Training Seminar (NLATS) and the Developing Youth Leaders Conference (DYLC) training opportunties in the Western Region.

NLS

The National Leadership Seminar (NLS) is a weekend training program focusing on leadership development. Run by the Region Chief and his Advisers, it provides an in-depth look at leadership skills and techniques.

DYLC

The Developing Youth Leadership Conference (DYLC) focuses on the tools every adult needs to mentor and develop our outstanding youth leaders.
